My Neighborhood

123 Address Street, River North

I've lived in River North since March 2017. I moved here for the convenience of being able to walk everywhere I need to go. Living in River North is busier than where I was living before in Nobel Square. But I like how much more there is within a 5 min walk.

My Favorite Neighborhood Sights

These are all places I frequently visit, but they all offer something different. East Bank I love to work out at. Three Greens has a great burger and is a good place to hang out. They always seem to be playing great movies. And I love the rotating cocktail list at Broken Shaker. The ambiance is also wonderful, I feel like I'm at a 70s beach house.

  • East Bank Club
  • Three Greens
  • Broken Shaker
